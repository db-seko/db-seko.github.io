1. Information we collect

We may collect:

  • Account and profile data — such as name, email, role, and preferences you provide when registering or using workspace features.

We use personal data to:

3. Legal bases (where applicable)

If your jurisdiction requires a legal basis (for example, the GDPR), we rely on one or more of: performance of a contract, legitimate interests (such as security and service improvement), consent where required, and legal obligation.

We may share information with:

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes above, including legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Retention periods may depend on the program or record type; contact us for specifics relevant to your account or submissions.

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies that are strictly necessary for the Services to function, and where applicable, optional cookies for analytics or preferences. You can control cookies through your browser settings.
