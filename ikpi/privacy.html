Privacy Policy

iKPi — Automated Performance Management

Last updated: March 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when using the performance management system, including your name, email, department, assessment data, and feedback. This information is used to support performance reviews and reporting within your organization.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to manage performance cycles, generate assessments, produce reports for managers and HR, and support the helpdesk. We do not sell or share your data with third parties outside your organization.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Access is restricted to authorized personnel and systems. Data is stored securely in Azure cloud infrastructure.

4. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Contact your HR administrator or system administrator for support.

5. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact your organization's HR or IT department.
